Mechanical and Bioengineering Systems Labratory

-Modeling, analysis and design for mechanical and bioengineering systems-

Division of Bioengineering, Department of Mechanical Science and Bioengineering

Graduate School of Engineering Science, Osaka University

Main Research Topics:

  • Modeling and analysis for orthopedic and orthodontic biomechanics
  • Human body motion analysis for design/evaluation of assistive rehabilitation devices
  • Optimality analysis of bone tissue/structure and design optimization of adaptive structural system
  • Assistant system for smart structure/mechanism design
